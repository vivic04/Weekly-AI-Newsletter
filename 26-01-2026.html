Executive Summary

The week of January 21-26, 2026, confirmed that AI is now a primary driver of bank earnings. Ally Financial reported a stunning 166% increase in EPS (Jan 23), explicitly citing their ally.ai platform for improved credit performance.

Policy pressures are mounting. With the CFPB's Section 1071 compliance deadline passing on Jan 16 and the EU AI Act's high-risk rules looming for August, banks are racing to harden their governance frameworks. This "Compliance Crunch" is driving a wedge between Tier 1 players who can automate reporting (JPM, Capital One) and smaller peers struggling with data overhead.
